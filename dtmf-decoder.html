DTMF decoder

Decodes DTMF audio signal to 4 bit binary TTL level output with LED indication. Suitable for use with microcontroller applications.

 

Features

 

* Decodes DTMF as 4 bit binary

* TTL level output for direct connection to microcontrollers

* Low power 5V DC operation

* LED indication of outputs

* Simple to use

* Based on excellent MT8870 IC
